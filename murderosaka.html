Murder in Osaka

A gripping murder mystery game set in Osaka, where you investigate a string of murders while evading a killer panda.

  • Full Story

About the Game

Murder in Osaka is an immersive detective game where you play as a skilled investigator solving a series of mysterious murders that have occurred in Osaka. As you gather clues, interrogate witnesses, and solve puzzles, you’ll uncover the dark secrets hidden in the city. But be careful — a killer panda is on the loose, adding a deadly twist to your investigation. This game combines atmospheric storytelling, challenging puzzles, and a thrilling narrative that will keep you on edge.

Game Features

  • Investigate crime scenes and gather crucial evidence
  • Engage with a cast of intriguing characters
  • Unravel the mystery with a captivating storyline
  • Beware of the unpredictable killer panda!
